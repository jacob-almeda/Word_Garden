title: As a Flower / A Seed’s A Star 
tag: poem
alignment: center

I wish I could be like a flower
But, alas, I am yet a bud
And in your heart
Is another star

So I grow in gentle quietude
Awaiting the day I may blossom
An eruption of life and a torrent of hues
Enough to rival the cosmos

But even when my body
Into dust, withers away
To drift across space, flowing and free
A seed is a star for another day
