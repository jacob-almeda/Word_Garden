title: As a Plant
tag: poem
alignment: center

I should like to become a plant
Quietly growing in faithful dignity
Never asking for more
Than what is already given to me
By nature’s grace

I should like to give and give and give
The breath of life that flows through you
Or perhaps a dependable visage of love
Never seeking recompense

Until this mortal body withers away
Or, bursting forth, blooms fragrantly
I am yours
As a plant
