The term 'memory foam' is quite wondrous, isn’t it?
As if the pillow itself were made of thoughts and dreams
Fostering a most perfect cradle
For us to drift into sweet sleep 

Or, even more literally
It’s considerate of the cushion
To be bent and crushed under our weight
And yet return to its original form
For the cycle to start all over again

By the same token
The phrase 'forgive and forget' is a bit dull
Yes, forgiveness is a boon, but forgetfulness is a prison
Where would we be without history?
Without the past informing our future steps?

The person, or people
Who coined the term 'forgive and forget'
Clearly never appreciated 'memory foam'
