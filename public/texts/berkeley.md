My Ate lives in a different world from me
In it, there are no definites
No hard and fast rules
And life becomes what you make of it

It’s a wondrous way of living
But that’s exactly why
It feels like I belong
In a separate world

One that moves more slowly
One that feels simpler
And more discrete/discreet

The idea troubles me to think
That we don’t belong in the same world
But that’s merely a human perception
A mortal, earthly perspective

For in the end
Love will eradicate division 
Reconcile the divided
And heal any troubled heart

It will usher all of our souls
Into luminous, eternal communion
Our souls
The true existences
