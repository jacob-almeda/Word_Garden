title: Over the Bay
tag: poem
alignment: center
---

Oh, what a joyous sight
Such a beautiful Day
The Sun rises in the east
And sets over the bay

The Sun stretches down her warm arms
To gently embrace the Earth
Her golden reflection, glistening 
Upon the rippling waters
Reaches forth like a solemn farewell
And reminds me of your love

Oh, but where has the light gone?
Where is the loving light of Day?
Alas, my beloved is gone from me
As Night steals the light away

Yet forevermore,
The Sun rises in the east
And sets over the bay
