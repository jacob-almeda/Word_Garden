title: Lo, The Moon!

Lo! The Moon
That unreachable gemstone of enduring beauty
Momentarily peeks out from behind dark curtains
And is veiled yet again

Alas, is my passion merely one of vain unattainability?
For I stretch and reach and yearn
Yet will never have

And if I were to grasp it somehow
That pure and brilliant jewel
Would my adoration become something else entirely?
Perhaps the very act of reaching
Is where meaning is found

If, in my seizing
My affection would cease to exist
I’d rather cast it all off
I’d rather grasp naught at all
